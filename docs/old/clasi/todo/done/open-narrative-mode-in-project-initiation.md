# Open narrative mode in project initiation interview

During the Product Manager's project initiation interview, one of the
options presented to the stakeholder should be to start an open narrative.
The client often just wants to talk through their project — turn on voice
mode and dictation mode and let them speak freely.

After the narrative, the agent should:

1. Read and internalize the stakeholder's narration.
2. Synthesize it into the structured documents (overview, use cases, etc.).
3. Continue the interview with any remaining clarifying questions that
   weren't covered in the narrative.

This makes the interview feel more natural and accommodates stakeholders
who think better out loud than in response to structured questions.
